üîß Troubleshooting

App Won't Load Manga

Solution: Check your internet connection. MangaKoi requires an internet connection to browse and search for manga. If you're offline, you can only access previously downloaded content.

Downloads Not Working

Solution: Ensure you have sufficient storage space and a stable internet connection. Downloads may be paused if your device is in Low Power Mode or if battery is low.

Slow Performance

Solution: Clear the app cache from Settings > Storage > Clear Cache. This will remove cached images and may improve performance.

Can't Disable Parental Lock

Solution: Parental lock requires biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode) to disable. Ensure biometric authentication is enabled in your device settings.

App Crashes or Freezes

Solution: Force close the app and restart it. If the problem persists, try clearing the cache or restarting your device.

Can't Find Specific Manga

Solution: Try different search terms or check if the manga is available on MangaDex. Some manga may not be available due to licensing restrictions.
